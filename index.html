Go to "Pernambuco"?
do not forget to visit these Beaches

The most beautiful beaches are in Pernambuco. Do not forget to visit them when you are there. You will be amazed at each of them.

Beaches

Calhetas

Surrounded by rocks and coconut palms is this beautiful setting. The sea bath is a delight in the warm and transparent waters. You will relax under the shade of the coconut trees or in nice restaurants. .

Carneiros

It is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in Brazil. Sheep is still wild and charming. With calm and warm waters practically all year round, it has as one of its main attractions the Chapel of St.

Porto de Galinhas

The natural pools of Porto de Galinhas are formed by clear and warm water, The area surrounded by hotels, resorts, bars and restaurants usually swells at night. besides estuaries, mangroves, The whole region is very frequented by tourists and surfers of diverse nationalities,

Maracaípe

Calm and warm waters make the children happy, while on the other side surfers enjoy the crest of the wave. Visitors also enjoy raft and kayak trips to observe seahorses, as well as contemplate an unforgettable sunset.Maracaípe is recognized as a beach rich in environmental reserves. .

Fernando de Noronha

it is easy to find stingrays, fish and turtles, as well as many corals and vegetation on the rocks. On the side of the beach natural pools are formed when the tide is low, being ideal to appreciate all the richness of the local biodiversity, due to the transparency of the water.

São José da Coroa Grande

One of the most frequented beaches in the summer of the south coast of Pernambuco makes a border with the beach of Maragogi of the state of Alagoas, one of the best known beaches in Brazil.Its name comes from the crowns - rock formations-- .
